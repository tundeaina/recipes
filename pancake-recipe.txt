Ingredients:
 - 1/8 cup butter
 - 1/2 cups powdered sugar
 - 1/3 cup milk
 - 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
 - 1   egg

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, mix all dry stuff.
  2. Fold in Egg and melted butter. Beat until light and fluffy.
  3. Split mixture into 2 and pour each part into a heated pan.
  4. Turn when golden brown. Serve warm
